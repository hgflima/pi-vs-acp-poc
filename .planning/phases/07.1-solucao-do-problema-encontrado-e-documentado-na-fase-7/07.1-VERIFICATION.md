### Gaps Summary

No gaps found. All 7 must-have truths are verified, all 5 artifacts exist and are substantive, all 3 key links are wired, and both requirement IDs (OAUTH-02, OAUTH-03) are satisfied. Phase 7.1 goal is fully achieved.

**Root cause resolved:** The `getApiKey` callback in `setup.ts` received `"openai-codex"` from pi-agent-core (after `resolvePiProvider` remapped `"openai"` to `"openai-codex"` for OAuth), but the credential store keys by canonical `"openai"`. `getActiveCredential("openai-codex")` returned `null`, causing silent failure. The `toStoreProvider()` reverse-map (commit fd9f6fac) fixed this with a single, targeted, non-gambiarra change.

**D-03 also landed:** The `case "message_end"` error-surfacing in `stream-adapter.ts` (commit d7d7eaa9) is a permanent improvement — it ensures future credential or upstream errors are surfaced to the client as `event: error` rather than swallowed silently. This improvement stands independently of the SC#3/SC#4 fix.
